Global Privacy Policy

Your data security is our highest priority.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal personal data required to process orders and provide academy services. This includes your name, email, and shipping address. Payment data is handled securely by our partners like PayPal and never stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used for order fulfillment, course access management, and internal platform improvements. We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

4. International Data Transfers

As a global platform, your data may be processed in multiple regions to ensure fast service. We comply with international data protection standards across all jurisdictions.

5. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, reach out to our team at privacy@maahirshop.com.
